Ormsby Guitars Growth Plan

Communication with Dealers

Strengthening relationships with global dealers and distributors.

  • Steps:
    1. Create an onboarding package that includes product catalogs, training materials, and promotional resources for new dealers.
    2. Schedule training sessions to educate dealers on Ormsby Guitars' unique selling points and customization options.
    3. Develop a system for dealers to place bulk orders easily through an online platform.
  • Expected Outcome: Improved dealer satisfaction and a more efficient onboarding process.

  • Steps:
    1. Send out quarterly newsletters with updates on new products, upcoming events, and promotional opportunities.
    2. Hold virtual meetings with key dealers to gather feedback and address any issues.
    3. Provide dealers with exclusive promotional materials to help them sell Ormsby products effectively.
  • Expected Outcome: Stronger communication with dealers, leading to better sales performance and collaboration.
