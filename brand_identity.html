Ormsby Guitars Growth Plan

Strengthening Brand Identity

Developing a cohesive and impactful brand presence.

  • Steps:
    1. Develop a content calendar for the "Behind the Scenes with Perry" series.
    2. Set up production for filming with quality equipment.
    3. Schedule regular filming sessions with Perry.
    4. Edit videos to create engaging content.
    5. Distribute videos across platforms like YouTube and social media.
    6. Engage with the audience through comments and feedback.
  • Expected Outcome: Increased engagement and a stronger emotional connection with the audience.

  • Steps:
    1. Audit current website and online platforms for brand consistency.
    2. Update visuals and copy according to new brand guidelines.
    3. Enhance user experience and navigation.
    4. Ensure mobile responsiveness and accessibility compliance.
    5. Beta test the new design with a focus group.
    6. Launch the updated platforms.
  • Expected Outcome: Improved user engagement and a unified brand presence online.

    1. Identify key social media platforms for target audiences.
    2. Create platform-specific content plans.
    3. Design visuals and write copy aligned with brand guidelines.
    4. Schedule regular posts and monitor engagement.
    5. Utilize social media advertising for broader reach.
    6. Collaborate with influencers and brand ambassadors.
  • Expected Outcome: Increased brand visibility and audience engagement on social platforms.

  • Steps:
    1. Conduct keyword research relevant to the industry.
    2. Develop a blog and article publication schedule.
    3. Create high-quality, SEO-optimized content.
    4. Integrate multimedia elements like images and videos.
    5. Promote content through newsletters and social media.

    1. Set up brand monitoring tools to track mentions and sentiment.

    3. Analyze data to identify areas for improvement.

Summary

This plan strengthens Ormsby Guitars' brand identity by leveraging Perry Ormsby's connection with the community and emphasizing digital strategies. Each step builds upon the previous one, creating a cohesive and strategic approach to expanding the brand's reach while ensuring consistency and authenticity.
