Ormsby Guitars Growth Plan

Production of Visual Content

Captivating our audience with high-quality visual storytelling.

  • Steps:
    1. Hire professional photographers to capture high-quality images of each guitar model.
    2. Set up a studio with appropriate lighting and backgrounds to highlight product details.
    3. Ensure that each guitar's unique features are showcased through detailed shots and creative angles.
  • Expected Outcome: Stunning product images that enhance the perceived value of Ormsby Guitars, leading to increased customer interest.

  • Steps:
    1. Produce short-form video content for Instagram and TikTok, showcasing guitar details and performance clips.
    2. Create tutorials, demos, and behind-the-scenes videos to engage and educate the audience.
    3. Optimize video content for each platform, including subtitles, captions, and appropriate aspect ratios.
  • Expected Outcome: Increased engagement and shareability of visual content across social media platforms, driving traffic back to the website.
